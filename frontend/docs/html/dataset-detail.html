About this dataset

The 2024 Health Facility Census is a structured inventory of every public, private, and faith-based healthcare facility operating in The Gambia. It captures ownership type, level of care (from health post through teaching hospital), service offerings, bed capacity, staffing levels by cadre, and geocoded coordinates verified via field GPS visits between January and November 2024.

It is the authoritative reference dataset behind the National Health Facility Map, the SDG 3.8 service-coverage indicator, and the annual budget allocation framework operated by the Ministry of Health and the Ministry of Finance.

Methodology

Each record was verified through a three-step process between January and November 2024:

  • Desk reconciliation against the 2023 register, the National Health Information System, and Pharmacy Council and Medical & Dental Council registries.
  • Field GPS visits by 18 enumerators, capturing coordinates with sub-10-metre accuracy on Trimble R1 receivers. 614 of 728 facilities (84%) were physically visited; the remainder were geocoded from existing trusted sources.
  • Inspection cross-check with the latest Health Facility Inspectorate report, flagging discrepancies for follow-up.

Field-level null rates above 5% are flagged in the schema table. Documented limitations are in the methodology appendix.

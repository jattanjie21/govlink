Brand assets · v1.4.2

The wordmark, mark, and a small considered nod to national identity.

A serif wordmark in Fraunces, anchored on a single deep-teal stripe — a quiet reference to printed-document authority. The Gambian flag appears once, deliberately, as an 8 px stripe across the OG card; nowhere else as UI chrome.

01

Wordmark

The primary mark. Fraunces semi-bold at -0.02em tracking, paired with a single 14×48 px deep-teal stripe sitting on the baseline. Use this on light surfaces; reverse the colors for dark.

05

Do's and don'ts

The wordmark is more restrained than most national-platform brands by design. Don't compensate by stretching, recolouring, or "fixing" it.

GovLink
Do · keep proportionsStripe is roughly 1/8 the cap height of the wordmark.
GovLink
Don't · sans-serif the wordmarkThe wordmark is set in Fraunces. Always.
GovLink
Don't · gradient anythingGradients break the editorial register. The single accent stays solid.
GovLink
Don't · swap the stripe for a circle / iconThe stripe is the mark. Don't replace it with a dot, an arrow, or an emoji.
GovLink
Don't · flag-as-wordmarkOne 8 px stripe across the OG card. The wordmark stays neutral.
GovLink
Don't · slant or italiciseItalic Fraunces is reserved for editorial decks; the wordmark is upright.
